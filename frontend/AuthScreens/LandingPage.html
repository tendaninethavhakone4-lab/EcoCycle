The Platform

Built for the people doing the real work.

From sidewalk to sorting facility — everything you need to digitize, verify, and scale waste recovery.

Verified Recovery

Tamper-proof logging for every gram of material. QR-scan, geo-stamp, weight capture — chain of custody by default.

Instant Payouts

Auto-settle pickers in seconds based on real-time market indices and material grades. No paperwork.

Enterprise Suite

Multi-branch governance, role-based access, and audit trails for organizations with 100+ collection points.

ESG Reporting

Institutional-grade dashboards for CO₂ offsets, circularity ratios, and stakeholder-ready impact reports.

Built-in Marketplace

Direct pipeline from informal recovery to industrial recycling buyers. List, negotiate, settle.

Eco Intelligence

Predictive AI for material flow, pricing volatility, and supply chain health. Stay ahead of the curve.
